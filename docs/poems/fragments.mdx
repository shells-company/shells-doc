---
title: "In Pieces"
description: "When the world explodes."
---

I feel like my world is exploding.

I hate it when the world explodes.\
Graduation day.\
All the fragments of the life I knew are gone.

Today feels like this,\
my friends are gone.\
Fragments scattering to the different edges of space.\
Do I cling to a fragment?\
Or do I go to my own place?

I am always "the last to leave"\
or the first of them.\
But is that always true?

If you leave someone, you'll always feel like you're the first.\
And if they need your goodbye, you're the last.

Do you just want more time?

They haven't entirely gone, but they've gone from the "me" that was always a work in progress.

That is the "me" who needed them.\
And now all that remains is —\
"may it not be an empty promise."
