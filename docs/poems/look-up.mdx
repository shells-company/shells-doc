---
title: "Look Up"
---

I find a note torn out that just says 02/17 2026.\
It's a hint.\
A paper trail of a time I wrote about,\
then signed and dated it,\
then changed my mind as a just in case.

I know what notebook this belongs to.\
I refuse to go down the rabbit hole this time.

I clean my desk.\
There's so much dirt I never noticed,\
so many fingernails left behind.\
I always felt like my nails were dirty.\
Little did I know how much I'd been cleaning them.

I order another pair of razors.\
Something to make shaving feel easier.\
It finally comes and I'm excited.\
I hop in the shower and find\
I already have so much shaving equipment.

Why did I need to buy another?\
Why did I never notice?\
Do I not look up in my own home?

Wait —\
I look up from the past written.\
I find a nail kit in my closet.\
I was so happy I found another set.

They don't disappear.

It's so simple:\
don't let people speak for you.\
Just do what you want.\
And if you want help,\
make a document asking for it.\
They will rally behind you\
if it's worth rallying for.

You haven't made a doc in a while.\
So you're just working on the editor.\
Finding a problem worth solving.

Private notes.\
Shared notes.

Now let's get coffee\
and take a break.
