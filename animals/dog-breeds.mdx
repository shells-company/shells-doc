## Introduction to Dog Breeds

Dogs have been companions to humans for thousands of years, and through selective breeding, we now have over 300 recognized breeds worldwide. Each breed has unique characteristics, temperaments, and care requirements.

## Popular Dog Breeds

### Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds in the world. Known for their friendly and outgoing nature, Labs make excellent family pets and working dogs.

| Characteristic | Details |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Size | Large (55-80 lbs) |
| Temperament | Friendly, Active, Outgoing |
| Life Span | 10-12 years |
| Coat | Short, dense, water-resistant |

<Tip>
Labradors are highly food-motivated, making them easy to train but also prone to obesity. Monitor their diet carefully!
</Tip>

### German Shepherd

German Shepherds are intelligent, versatile, and loyal dogs. They excel in various roles including police work, search and rescue, and as family protectors.

| Characteristic | Details |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Size | Large (50-90 lbs) |
| Temperament | Confident, Courageous, Smart |
| Life Span | 9-13 years |
| Coat | Double coat, medium length |

### Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are beloved for their gentle, friendly nature and beautiful golden coats. They are excellent with children and make wonderful therapy dogs.

| Characteristic | Details |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Size | Large (55-75 lbs) |
| Temperament | Friendly, Reliable, Trustworthy |
| Life Span | 10-12 years |
| Coat | Long, flowing, water-repellent |

### Bulldog

Bulldogs are known for their wrinkled face and stocky build. Despite their tough appearance, they are gentle and affectionate companions.

| Characteristic | Details |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Size | Medium (40-50 lbs) |
| Temperament | Calm, Courageous, Friendly |
| Life Span | 8-10 years |
| Coat | Short, smooth |

<Warning>
Bulldogs are brachycephalic (flat-faced) and can have breathing difficulties. They should avoid strenuous exercise in hot weather.
</Warning>

### Beagle

Beagles are small hounds known for their incredible sense of smell and friendly disposition. They are curious, merry, and make great family pets.

| Characteristic | Details |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Size | Small to Medium (20-30 lbs) |
| Temperament | Curious, Friendly, Merry |
| Life Span | 12-15 years |
| Coat | Short, dense |

## Choosing the Right Breed

When selecting a dog breed, consider the following factors:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Lifestyle Assessment">
    Evaluate your activity level, living space, and daily schedule to find a breed that matches your lifestyle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Size Considerations">
    Consider how much space you have and whether you can handle a larger dog physically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grooming Requirements">
    Some breeds require extensive grooming while others are low-maintenance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Health Considerations">
    Research breed-specific health issues and associated veterinary costs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Breed Categories

Dogs are typically categorized into groups based on their original purpose:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sporting Group" icon="bullseye">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Working Group" icon="hammer">
    Bred for tasks like guarding, pulling sleds, and rescue. Examples: Boxers, Great Danes, Huskies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Herding Group" icon="sheep">
    Bred to control the movement of livestock. Examples: Border Collies, German Shepherds, Corgis.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Toy Group" icon="heart">
