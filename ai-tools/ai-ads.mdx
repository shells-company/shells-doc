Use AI Ads to help write and maintain your documentation. This guide shows how to configure AI Ads for better results on technical writing tasks and using Mintlify components.

## Prerequisites

- AI Ads installed
- Access to your documentation repository

## Getting started

<Steps>
<Step title="Install AI Ads">
  Download and install AI Ads from the official website.
</Step>

<Step title="Connect your repository">
  Open AI Ads and connect it to your documentation repository.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure settings">
  Set up your preferences for documentation generation and editing.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Content generation" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">

<Card title="Review assistance" icon="magnifying-glass">

## Best practices

<Tip>
Use AI Ads alongside your existing writing workflow rather than as a complete replacement. Review all generated content before publishing.
</Tip>

- Start with an outline before generating content
- Provide context about your target audience
- Review and edit AI-generated content for accuracy
- Use the style guide feature to maintain consistency
