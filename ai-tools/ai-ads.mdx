## Key features

### Automated campaign management

AI handles the time-consuming aspects of campaign management:

- **Bid optimization** - Automatically adjusts bids based on performance data
- **Audience targeting** - Identifies and targets high-converting segments
- **Budget allocation** - Distributes spend across campaigns for maximum ROI
- **A/B testing** - Runs and analyzes tests without manual setup

## Best practices

<Tip>
Start with AI handling one campaign type, then expand as you see results. This lets you validate the productivity gains before full adoption.
</Tip>

1. **Review AI suggestions weekly** - While AI handles execution, periodic review ensures alignment with business goals
2. **Set clear guardrails** - Define budget limits and brand guidelines for AI to follow
3. **Track time savings** - Measure hours saved to quantify productivity improvements
4. **Iterate on goals** - Update your targets as AI learns and improves performance
