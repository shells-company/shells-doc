AI Ads uses machine learning to optimize ad targeting, helping you reach the right users at the right time.

## How it works

1. **Data collection** - The platform analyzes user behavior, demographics, and engagement patterns
2. **Model training** - AI models learn which audience segments convert best for your campaigns
3. **Real-time optimization** - Targeting adjusts automatically based on performance data

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a campaign">
    Navigate to the dashboard and click **New Campaign**. Select your campaign objective.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your audience">
    Set initial targeting parameters. The AI will refine these as it learns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload creatives">
    Add your ad creatives. Enable **Auto-optimize** to let AI test variations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set budget and launch">
    Configure your daily budget and bid strategy, then launch your campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- **Start broad** - Let the AI discover optimal segments rather than over-constraining targeting
- **Provide conversion data** - The more conversion signals you track, the better the AI performs
- **Allow learning time** - Give campaigns 7-14 days before making major changes
- **Test multiple creatives** - Upload at least 3-5 variations for effective optimization
