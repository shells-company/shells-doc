## Potential sounds

Foxes make a variety of vocalizations:

- **Ring-ding-ding-ding-dingeringeding** - A playful call
- **Wa-pa-pa-pa-pa-pa-pow** - An excited bark
- **Hatee-hatee-hatee-ho** - A territorial cry
- **Joff-tchoff-tchoffo-tchoffo-tchoff** - A mating call
- **Jacha-chacha-chacha-chow** - A warning sound
- **Fraka-kaka-kaka-kaka-kow** - An alarm call
- **A-hee-ahee ha-hee** - A howl
- **A-oo-oo-oo-ooo** - A long-distance communication
- **Woo-oo-oo-ooo** - A nighttime vocalization

In reality, foxes commonly make these sounds:

- **Barking** - Short, sharp yaps
- **Screaming** - High-pitched shrieks, especially during mating season
- **Howling** - Long, drawn-out calls
- **Gekkering** - Chattering sounds during fights or play
- **Whining** - Submissive vocalizations
